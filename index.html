About Us

At St.Thomas Deco by Raj, we believe every celebration deserves to be extraordinary. Based in St. Thomas, Ontario, we specialize in turning your vision into stunning reality — from intimate baby showers to grand wedding receptions.

With a passion for design and an eye for detail, we craft personalized decor that reflects your unique style. Whether it's lush balloon arches, elegant floral arrangements, or complete event styling, we bring creativity, care, and professionalism to every event.
